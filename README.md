# Monthly Sales Growth Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes monthly sales performance using Power BI to help management monitor short-term sales dynamics, identify growth trends, and support strategic decision-making.

The dashboard provides insights into:

* Monthly Sales Growth
* Total Sales Performance
* Regional Sales Analysis
* Product Category Performance
* Salesperson Performance
* Quarterly Sales Trends

---

## Tools Used

* Power BI
* Excel

---

## Selected KPI

### Monthly Sales Growth %

Measures the month-over-month increase in sales revenue.

Formula:

Monthly Sales Growth % =
(Current Month Sales - Previous Month Sales) / Previous Month Sales × 100

---

## Dashboard Features

* KPI Cards
* Sales Trend Analysis
* Quarterly Comparison
* Regional Performance Analysis
* Product Category Breakdown
* Salesperson Performance Tracking
* Interactive Filters

---

## Key Insights

* Sales showed a steady upward trend over time.
* Electronics generated the highest percentage of total sales.
* The North region achieved the strongest sales performance.
* Monthly sales growth remained positive, indicating healthy business expansion.
* The dashboard enables faster and more informed business decisions.

---

## Business Objective

The main objective of this dashboard is to provide management with a short-term performance indicator instead of relying only on quarterly or yearly reports.

This helps:

* Anticipate market demand
* Optimize sales strategies
* Track performance changes quickly
* Support team target planning
